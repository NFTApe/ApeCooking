Easy Brownie Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Sugar (2 cups)
  • All-purpose Flour (1½ cups)
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Walnut(Optional)

How To Make

Step 1

1.Pre-heat the oven to 350°C (temperature may vary depending on your oven/microwave size).

Step 2

2.Mix the sugar, flour, melted butter, cocoa powder, vanilla extract, baking powder, and salt in a bowl. Spread the mixture onto a baking sheet and add the optional walnuts.

Step 3

3.Bake till the top of it is a little hard. The cake should be expanding.Take it out of the oven/microwave, let it cool, and enjoy!
